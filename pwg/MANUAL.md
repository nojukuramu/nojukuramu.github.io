PINOY WORDGAME MANUAL — LETTER-SHIFT SERIES
=============================================

CORE TYPES & RULES
-------------------

DL — DAGDAG LETRA
  Word 1 + letter(s), ADDED AT THE END = Word 2
  Both words must be valid Tagalog/Filipino words.

  Q: MAGANDANG UMAGA ng ANYONG TUBIG
  A: BATI - BATIS

BL — BAWAS LETRA
  Word 1 − letter(s), REMOVED FROM THE END = Word 2
  Both words must be valid.

  Q: SAKTO ng ANYONG TUBIG
  A: SAPAT - SAPA

KL — KISLAP LETRA
  Word 1 → Word 2: SAME LENGTH, letter(s) swapped at the SAME POSITION(S)

  Q: sa KAPATID ko PINAGAWA
  A: UTOL - UTOS

BS — BANAT SALITA
  Word 1 split into PREFIX + SUFFIX; insert letters in the GAP = Word 2

  Q: KINAMOT na HAYOP
  A: KATI - KALAPATI

VARIANTS
----------
  Applies to DL, BL, and KL only (BS already has flexible insertion length, no variant needed).
  The number of letters added/removed/swapped is not fixed at 1 or 2 — it can be 1, 2, 3, or more,
  depending on difficulty. Just label the variant accordingly (e.g. 3DL, 3BL, 3KL) and follow the
  same positional rule per category (end for DL/BL, same position for KL).

CLUE FORMAT (flexible, pick any)
----------------------------------
  - Simple 2-clue pair: one hint per word
  - Witty single sentence combining both meanings
  - Short riddle/poem weaving both words together
  Requirement: both target words must be inferable from the clue.

ANSWER FORMAT
---------------
  Always present as: WORD1 - WORD2
  No annotations, brackets, or letter breakdowns.

NOTATION SUMMARY
-------------------
  DL   = +N letter(s), end
  BL   = −N letter(s), end
  KL   = swap N letter(s), same position(s)
  BS   = insert letters mid-word (split prefix/suffix)
  N    = 1, 2, 3, or more (specify per item, e.g. 2DL, 3KL)
